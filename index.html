About Us

The Misamis Oriental Institute of Science and Technology (MOIST) is an institution offering all educational levels – nursery, elementary, secondary, tertiary and vocational courses. It first began operating in the year 2002 through the triumvirate initiative of Mr. Enrico Valmores; Mr. Reynaldo Valmores, RPh; and the late Atty. Romualdo Valmores. The school is exactly located in Sta. Cruz (a bypass road), Cogon, Balingasag – an agricultural municipality in the province of Misamis Oriental. Due to its affordable education, the institution has the greatest number of student populace in all the schools in the municipality. Most of the students of the institution are nearby residents in the town, some come from its neighboring towns, and some other are from its bordering provinces taking the risk to live in independence. With the goal to relevant and quality education that brings about economic progress in the community where the learners are a part of, the institution is continuously in pursuit to provide its learners holistic education that will lead them to professional development and personal maturity through the aid and contributions of science and technology, skill-based assessments and activities in and outside of the four-cornered classroom, research studies at an early level, and flexible and guided learning modality.
